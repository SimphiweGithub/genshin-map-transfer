How to Get Your HoYoLAB Cookies in 30 Seconds

  1. Go to hoyolab.com and sign in to your account.
  2. Press F12 (or right-click and choose Inspect) to open the browser Developer Tools.
  3. Go to the Application tab (Chrome/Edge) or Storage tab (Firefox).
  4. In the left sidebar, expand Cookies and select https://www.hoyolab.com.
  5. Look for ltoken_v2 and ltuid_v2 in the table. Copy their values and paste them above. (If you only see ltoken and ltuid, copy those instead).
⚠️ Security Note: Your credentials are saved only inside your browser's local storage. They are sent directly to your Vercel/local API proxy and are never stored on any database. Do not share your cookies with others.
